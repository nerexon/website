Our projects

KeychainDB

KeychainDB is a key-value database for Node.js applications. It allow to save data localy with breaking your head while configuring a server or a complexe database.

  • Learn more

LangPack

LangPack or Language Pack is a language management system for Node.js applications. It allow translation of text into multiple languages by using simple JSON files. It is compatible with KeychainDB.

  • Learn more

Nerix Comming soon

Nerix is a web server wrote in Go and Javascript (Node.js) made to be fast and simple. It allow the deployement of web applications in minutes. It supports a large variety of languages and frameworks.

About us

Who are we ?

We are a companie dedicated in the creation of open source projects. From web servers to databases, we have a bit of everything for you. If you ever have any project idea, you are free to contact us.

Our commitments

We commit ourself to make exclusively open source software while maintaining transparent communication with our customers. We are dedicated to the quality and safety of our products.

Our mission

Our mission is to contribute to the world of open source software and make life easier for developers. We do not just create software for developers, we are also very active in the community.

Our motto

Our motto is “Welcome to our open and better world”. It represents the world of free software, which we actively support, as well as our fight for freedom of expression.

Our guarantees

We are committed to offering the best possible software quality and providing the best possible customer support. For us, the customer comes first and always will.

Our team

We have a team of people who are passionate about their work and their contributions to open source software. They are willing to do anything to fulfill our goals and create a better world.
